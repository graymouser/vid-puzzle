HTML5 Video Puzzle

A humble internet project by Joshua Shammay [e]
Because videos and puzzles are what the internet is about!

Instructions: Click and drag tiles to move them, blue tile border disappears on being placed in correct position.

The source for this project can be viewed at github.com/graymouser/vid-puzzle

Videos are (to the best of my knowledge) in the public domain, and were obtained from the Internet Archive
